Why peakit

peakit is a planning aid for hikers, ski tourers and mountaineers. Bring together the forecast, slope angle & aspect, sun & shadow, and the trails that get you there — all in one map you can take offline.

Upgrade to peakit Pro for the 7-day forecast, unlimited offline regions, sun & shadow overlay, route elevation profiles, GPX export, Meshtastic group tracking and quick access to EAWS avalanche bulletins.

🗺️

Outdoor maps, fully offline

MapTiler outdoor tiles with contour lines & hillshading. Download regions ahead of time — the map, slope shading and forecast all work offline in the field.

🌤️

Forecast with elevation snap

Weather from Open-Meteo, snapped to the elevation of the point you tap so you're reading the summit forecast, not the valley one.

▲

Slope shading & sunPro

Avalanche-scale slope-angle colour ramp, plus per-face sun & shadow with a time slider. Understand the terrain and the light before you commit.

🥾

Routes & hiking trails

Import GPX, draw a route on the map, or snap between waypoints along real hiking trails via Open Route Service. Hiking network overlay via Waymarked Trails.

📡

Meshtastic group trackingPro

Pair a Meshtastic node over Bluetooth to see where your party is, share waypoints and message each other — no cell signal required.

⚠️

Avalanche bulletinsPro

Quick access to the current EAWS avalanche bulletin, right from the Layers menu. Always cross-check the official source before backcountry travel.

Data sources

Weather from Open-Meteo. Map tiles & terrain elevation from MapTiler. Trails & place search from © OpenStreetMap contributors via Overpass and Waymarked Trails. Routing by Open Route Service. Avalanche bulletins via EAWS / avalanche.report (CC BY 4.0).
